# Core Enterprise Profiles of China's AI Computing Power Liquid Cooling Supply Chain  
  
> As of July 2026, China's liquid-cooled server market is estimated at approximately RMB 24 billion, with an annual growth rate exceeding 40%. The top four enterprises (Envicool, Sugon Digital Creation, Shenling Environment, and Exaton) dominate the primary "whitelists" for internet and server manufacturers. This report organizes listed, Pre-IPO, and specialized "Little Giant" enterprises across four sub-sectors: cold plate/immersion liquid cooling systems, CDUs, quick-connect couplings, and thermal interface materials.

## III. Thermal Interface Materials (TIM) – Supplementary Note  
  
Detailed information on enterprises specifically engaged in thermal interface materials (TIM) for AI computing power liquid cooling applications (e.g., thermal grease, thermal pads, phase-change materials) **was not obtained** in the current search materials.  
  
> **Industry Background Note**: TIM represents a critical thermal conduction link between liquid cooling systems and chips. Major domestic participants include **Zhongshi Technology** (300684.SZ, thermal/shielding materials), **Feirongda** (300602.SZ, EMI shielding and thermal materials), **Boway Alloy**, among others. However, the high-end AI computing power liquid cooling market still partially relies on imports (e.g., Honeywell, Laird). For further coverage of the TIM sector, targeted research on the liquid cooling business progress of the aforementioned enterprises is recommended.

## IV. Key Industry Summary – Liquid Cooling  
  
| Dimension | Core Findings |  
|------|----------|  
| **Consolidated Leadership Structure** | Envicool, Sugon Digital Creation, Shenling Environment, and Exaton constitute the first-tier "whitelist" cohort; Matthew effect is pronounced. |  
| **Significant Revenue Disparities** | Inspur accounts for 46.9% share with RMB 164.7 billion revenue, creating vast gaps with trailing enterprises. |  
| **Profitability Under Pressure** | Combined 2025 net profit of 35 liquid cooling concept companies increased only marginally by 1.9%; profits declined for half of enterprises, indicating industry is in a "high investment, slow monetization" adjustment phase. |  
| **Accelerated Domestic Substitution** | Sugon Digital Creation led formulation of China's first quick-connect coupling industry standard, advancing standardization and localization of liquid cooling supply chains. |  
| **Overseas Expansion** | Exaton (six Southeast Asian regions), Sugon Digital Creation (Singapore subsidiary) accelerating global expansion. |  
| **Geopolitical Risks** | Supermicro incident impacting Taiwan-based supply chain (Kaori); U.S. sanctions remain a core industry variable. |
