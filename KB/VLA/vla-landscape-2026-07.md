- 智平方 (AI² Robotics): ~$70M USD (~¥500M) order, 1,000+ units over 3 years from display maker HKC (001308.SZ). Largest known VLA deployment contract. Also delivered in automotive, semiconductor, biotech.

- DobotWAM (Kongyi): 99.25% avg on LIBERO benchmark — Spatial 99/100, Object 100/100, Goal 99/100, LIBERO-10 99/100. (Sources: Securities Times 2026-05-31; IT之家; Sina)

**Tier 3 — platform enablers:**

- 星海图 (XingHaiTu): G0 Plus/Tiny, out-of-the-box open-source VLA.
