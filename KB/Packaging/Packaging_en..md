### 4. Key Industry Risk Alerts  
  
1. **SJ Semiconductor Entity List Risk**: As the core enterprise holding 85% market share in domestic 2.5D packaging, SJ Semiconductor remains on the U.S. Entity List, with restrictions on imports of key equipment and raw materials, which may constrain capacity expansion pace. Its revenue concentration risk is prominent, with single largest customer accounting for as high as 74% of revenue.  
  
2. **Glass Substrate Industrialization Uncertainty**: As a next-generation packaging base material, glass substrate remains in early industrialization stage. BOE A's announcement explicitly states that related business is still in technology exploration and validation phase, with no mass production revenue generated.  
  
3. **Continued Escalation of Geopolitical Tensions**: In September 2025, U.S. BIS added 23 Chinese entities to the Entity List, primarily comprising 13 semiconductor companies. China's Ministry of Commerce has initiated anti-discrimination investigations; subsequent domestic substitution and supply chain security will become the main theme, but imports of advanced packaging equipment and materials will still face uncertainties in the short term.  
  
4. **Elevated Valuation Levels**: Since 2026, the advanced packaging sector has accumulated peak gains exceeding 200%; SJ Semiconductor's post-listing P/E valuation is approximately 275x, with the industry overall trading in a high-valuation range; attention should be paid to the pace of earnings realization.
