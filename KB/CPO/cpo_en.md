## Industry Key Developments  
  
- **TSMC silicon photonics integration platform expected to reach mass production in 2026**, serving as a key milestone for driving CPO technology from concept to commercial deployment  
- **Goldman Sachs CPO Supply Chain Map** indicates NVIDIA and Broadcom as core suppliers of CPO Switch ASICs; TSMC and ASE listed as key players in packaging segment; Coherent and Lumentum identified as primary suppliers of optical engines and laser light sources  
- **CPO switches expected to scale post-2027-2028 upon process maturity**; LPO/NPO routes currently offer greater engineering feasibility

### 4. Industry Key Risk Disclosures  
  
1. **SJ Semiconductor Entity List Risk**: As the core enterprise holding 85% market share in domestic 2.5D packaging, SJ Semiconductor currently remains on the U.S. Entity List, with restrictions on imports of key equipment and materials, potentially constraining capacity expansion pace. Its single largest customer accounts for 74% of revenue, highlighting significant customer concentration risk.  
  
2. **Glass Substrate Industrialization Uncertainty**: As next-generation packaging base material, glass substrate remains in early industrialization phase. BOE announcement explicitly states related business remains in technology exploration and validation phase, with no mass production revenue generated.  
  
3. **Continued Geopolitical Escalation**: In September 2025, U.S. BIS added 23 Chinese entities to Entity List, primarily comprising 13 semiconductor enterprises. China's Ministry of Commerce has initiated anti-discrimination investigation; subsequent domestic substitution and supply chain security will become main themes, though imports of advanced packaging equipment and materials face near-term uncertainties.  
  
4. **Elevated Valuation Levels**: Advanced packaging sector has accumulated peak gains exceeding 200% since 2026; SJ Semiconductor post-listing P/E valuation approximately 275x; industry overall in high-valuation range, warranting attention to earnings realization pace.
